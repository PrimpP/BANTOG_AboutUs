About Us





Who we are?

We are a group of eight students from the University of East Manila who are passionate about promoting Filipino cuisine. It's a privilege for us to have this opportunity to showcase across the world our cuisines. We spend time gathering information to ensure that our website visitors will have the best experience. We are proud to be Filipino. Mabuhay!


Our Purpose

We created this page to demonstrate how delicious Filipino cuisine is and to share the recipe with others so they may try it at home. This page will provide information and instructions on how to prepare different Filipino dishes. Those who wish to try Filipino cuisine on their own can do so by visiting our website and selecting from a large selection of dishes.
